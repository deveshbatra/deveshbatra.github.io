A personal website is a small place with a useful job: make the right links easy to find.

LinkedIn is good for professional context. Scholar is good for citations. GitHub is good for code. A homepage can be the quiet index that joins those things together, adds a little taste, and keeps the writing editable.

The ambition here is modest: papers, notes, projects, and a few small tools that should not need a platform strategy to exist.
